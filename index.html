Production blocked. The next commit 0988accf8 ("[SYCL] Q8_0 reorder ~3x speedup") breaks real generation — produces gibberish in /v1/chat/completions even though the bench passes. SYCL build frozen at b54cb2e3d.

Why SYCL is so much faster: it activates full XMX on the B70 (FP16 + BF16 + INT8 native), vs Vulkan which only has KHR_coopmat FP16. The 2–3× TG gap is entirely due to this.
